Why Accounting?

I decided to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Accounting for a few reasons. First, I really enjoy analytical thinking and problem-solving, especially when it involves working with numbers and finding efficient solutions. Accounting also offers great career stability, plus the flexibility to work almost anywhere since every business needs an accountant. There are tons of growth opportunities too, whether it's through specialization, moving into leadership roles, or even branching out into areas like finance or consulting. What I love about accounting is the variety; it keeps things interesting and gives me room to keep learning and growing throughout my career.

Key Interests:

  • Analytical Thinking/Problem Solving
  • Career Stability
  • Growth Opportunities
  • Variety in Career

Career Objectives:

I’m not exactly sure what specific path I want to take within accounting yet, but I definitely know I want to earn my CPA. I’m excited to keep growing in the field, explore different areas to find where I fit best, and build strong connections as I go.
