About Me

Get to know me better!

Background

Hi! My name is Celeste Blahoski. I am currently a sophomore college student at the University of Minnesota Duluth. I have lived in many different states, including Florida, Colorado, and Oklahoma, but Minnesota is where I have stayed the longest, and where I most feel at home. I am currently studying and am dedicated to accounting. I enjoy working with numbers, puzzles, and building relationships. I hope to commit to tax, so I can continue to use my skills to do volunteer work for those who do not understand how to do their taxes. It may be surprising to some, but I discovered (after growing up low-income myself) that many people from low-income families/neighborhoods never learned how to do their taxes.

Gaming Passion

Putting my future career and goals aside, My favorite thing to do is to indulge in gaming! I enjoy digital gaming and playing all sorts of games: RPGs, shooters, and simulation. I also really enjoy board games, my most recent favorite is called "Root" where you play as woodland creatures trying to take over the meadow. I enjoy this game each character has their own set of rules and goals to win, and you as a player have to spend a lot of time strategizing to earn your victory. I love the challenge and creativity a lot of games have, and often will spend all night playing, getting lost in the worlds someone has created.

Reading

Another hobby I have is Reading. If you couldn't tell already, I am very much a homebody. I don't have a specific genre that I prefer; I can always find something to love about a story. But if I had to choose an author, it would definitely be Cormac McCarthy. I love the postapocalyptic style and the way he adds a spiritual element to his stories that can be interpreted differently by each reader.
